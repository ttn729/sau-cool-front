About Hidden Words

Motivation

Original website link: Original Hidden Words

Hidden words was originally created to help language instructors prepare fill in the blank worksheets for their students. The idea is instructors will fill in two columns corresponding to the vocabulary words the students are learning and the meanings of those words.

This version of hidden words has more up to date styling, and exists along other useful tools compared to the standalone app I made before.

Features

The key feature of hidden words is the ability to randomly hide words and meanings, with the intent for the student to fill in these blanks as a vocabulary exercise.

The printer friendly button toggles a printer friendly view of a worksheet, that instructors can easily print using Ctrl-p. Why not construct a pdf and download it for the instructor? The instructor I was working with wanted to be able to quickly use the same vocabulary word set to create multiple different copies for each of her students. She was also concerned about having a lot of files cluttering her computer so this seemed to be the most optimal solution for her use case.

The number of lines to append adds lines at the end of the worksheet, allowing students to write down additional vocabulary words or focus on vocabulary words they are having difficulty with. The idea was to utilize the leftover white space on the page for this purpose. Unfortunately, this developer could not think of a way to dynamically determine how many extra lines are required to fill the rest of the page. For now, the number of lines to append exists as a way for instructors to manually add however many lines they want to add to the end of their vocabular words.

The number of words to keep allows instructors to make worksheets using only a partial amount of words from word pack.

The save button allows instructors to save their list of word meaning pairs into a word pack, that can be accessed later. In this way, instructors only have to enter in the data for their vocabulary lessons once and are able to make worksheets anytime with ease.
